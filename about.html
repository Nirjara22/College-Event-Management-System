About College Event Management System

Purpose

This document specifies the software requirements for the College Event Management System (CEMS) used for the INSIGHT and TECHNEX festivals at St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur. CEMS Version 1.0 provides an integrated solution for managing online event registration, scheduling, participant coordination, venue booking, communication (email/SMS), and result processing.

Project Scope

The College Event Management System (CEMS) is a web- and mobile-based platform designed to automate and optimize all aspects of college events. Organizers, faculty, and student coordinators can plan, schedule, promote, and manage technical and cultural activities—INSIGHT and TECHNEX—through a unified interface.

  • Online event & program registration with real-time capacity tracking and wait-lists.
  • Role-based panels for administrators, volunteers, and participants.
  • Automated notifications (email/SMS) for confirmations, schedule changes, and announcements.
  • Integrated payment gateway for paid workshops and events, with digital certificate issuance.
  • Analytics & reporting dashboard for attendance, feedback, and financial summaries.

Overview

CEMS replaces manual spreadsheets and fragmented tools with a single, scalable solution. It supports user classes such as students, organizers, guest speakers, and vendors—each with tailored access—and integrates with college systems for authentication, calendar syncing, and certificate generation. The system is built to handle peaks of up to 5,000 concurrent users, ensuring 99.9% uptime during major festival days.
